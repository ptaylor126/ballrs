# Privacy Policy

**Ballrs**

Last updated: December 27, 2025

---

## Overview

Ballrs is a sports trivia game. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information you provide
- **Username** - Required to play. Visible to other players on leaderboards and in duels.
- **Email and password** - Optional. Only if you choose to link an email for account recovery.

### Information collected automatically
- **Game data** - Your scores, streaks, XP, level, puzzle history, duel results, league memberships, and friends list.
- **Device token** - If you enable notifications, we store your push notification token to send you alerts.
- **Basic device info** - Device type and operating system for app functionality.

### Information we do NOT collect
- Location data
- Contacts
- Photos
- Payment information
- Advertising identifiers

---

## How We Use Your Information

- **To run the game** - Store your progress, display leaderboards, match you in duels, manage leagues and friends.
- **To send notifications** - Daily puzzle reminders, duel challenges, friend requests (only if you enable notifications).
- **To improve the app** - Understand how players use features so we can make the game better.

---

## How We Share Your Information

### Visible to other players
- Your username
- Your level and title
- Your position on leaderboards
- Your league memberships

### Service providers
We use third-party services to operate Ballrs:
- **Supabase** - Database and authentication (stores your account and game data)
- **Expo** - Push notifications (receives your device token to deliver notifications)

These providers only access data necessary to provide their services.

### We do NOT
- Sell your personal information
- Share your email with anyone
- Use your data for advertising
- Give third parties access to your game history

---

## Data Retention

- Your game data is kept as long as you have an account.
- If you delete your account, your data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
- Anonymous accounts that are inactive for 12 months may be deleted.

---

## Your Rights

### Access and export
You can view your stats and game history in the app at any time.

### Delete your account
Contact us at hello@ballrs.net to request account deletion. We'll delete all your data within 30 days.

### Notifications
You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

---

## Children's Privacy

Ballrs is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

---

## Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure database storage.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date.
