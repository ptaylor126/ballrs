How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Ballrs app
  2. Go to Profile (tap the profile icon in the bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll down to Settings
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm when prompted

What Gets Deleted

This permanently deletes your account and all associated data including:

  • Profile information and username
  • Gameplay stats and streaks
  • Friends list
  • League memberships
  • Duel history
  • XP and achievements
Warning: Data is deleted immediately and cannot be recovered.
